Biography

Tyler-Justin Anthony Sharpe, known professionally as Lil Tecca, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. He rose to mainstream fame with the release of his 2019 single "Ransom", which peaked at number four on the Billboard Hot 100. The song preceded the release of his debut mixtape We Love You Tecca (2019), which peaked at number four on the Billboard 200 and contained the follow-up singles "Love Me" and "Did It Again". :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

In 2023, Lil Tecca released his third studio album, TEC, which showcased a more personal and refined sound. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6} He followed up with Plan A in 2024, and as of April 2025, he is preparing to release his fifth studio album, DOPAMINE, accompanied by a celebratory tour. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}
